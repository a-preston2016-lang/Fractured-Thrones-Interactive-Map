Eshma Thalas

Eshma Thalas is the secluded woodland kingdom of the elves, ruled since the Second Era by the venerable House Aieiindar. Hidden beneath an immense canopy of ancient trees, its marble towers and vine-covered fortresses blend seamlessly into the deepwood, reflecting the elves’ devotion to harmony and secrecy. Shaped by a long history of conflict—from guerilla campaigns against Arilaand to the devastating clash with the newly rediscovered Drow — the kingdom has grown wary and insular, allowing no foreigner beyond its coastal docks. Under King Lillandril Aieiindar, a respected but aging warrior-king with no heir, the elves stand vigilant against rising threats, torn between old grievances and the looming possibility of war. Though graceful and ordered, Eshma Thalas carries a growing tension beneath its tranquil surface, as rival Houses debate the kingdom’s path and long-held traditions strain against the pressures of a changing world.
