Arilaand

Arilaand sits in the fertile heartlands of Auzera and has long been regarded as the historical cradle of Human civilization on the continent. Known for its rich farmlands, extensive forests, and strategic central location, Arilaand was once among the most powerful Human kingdoms, boasting strong trade with neighboring peoples and resisting many threats over the Ages. In present eras, it remains Human-led from its capital in Adwick under King Aleus Harwin, though its glory days are often contrasted against ongoing strife and external pressures.
