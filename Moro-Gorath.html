Moro-Gorath

Unlike the more civically organized kingdoms, Moro-Gorath is a desolate, cursed region of ancient ruins — once hosting a long-forgotten civilization. Now scarred by blackened stone, petrified remains, and hostile terrain, this land is shunned by most but visited by daring explorers seeking arcane relics. Dark forces and dangerous creatures are said to inhabit Moro-Gorath’s depths, making it one of Auzera’s most feared territories.
