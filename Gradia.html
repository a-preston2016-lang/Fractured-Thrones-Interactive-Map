Gradia

Gradia is celebrated as the wealthiest of the Human realms, strategically positioned on the coast with access to both maritime trade routes and rare resources found in nearby northern swamps. Its cities host some of the largest shipyards in Auzera, and its leadership — a council of powerful families headed by Baron Ottmar Giraldus — prides itself on tolerance, drawing diverse peoples to its ports. Gradia’s wealth and cosmopolitan nature make it a vibrant hub of commerce and culture.
