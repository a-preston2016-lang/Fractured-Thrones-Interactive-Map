Vurnfalduhr

Vurnfalduhr is a dwarven mountainous kingdom, rich in mineral wealth and famed for its grand subterranean halls. Historically prosperous and isolated, its clans now face brutal conflict at the fringes of its domain — particularly from Orc warbands originating in adjacent lands. Under High King Strorbren Hammerbane, Vurnfalduhr’s hardy dwarf folk continue a desperate defense to protect their ancestral strongholds while maintaining a proud but strained independence from outside allies.
